Everything you need to manage your network

From casual contacts to key relationships, Nexus gives you the tools to be intentional about your connections.

Contact Management

Photos, birthdays, custom fields, social links, location, and notes. Everything about a person in one place.

Keep In Touch

Set contact frequencies — weekly, monthly, quarterly. Get nudged when relationships go cold so you never lose touch.

Pipeline Board

Kanban view of your network organized by recency. See who you've talked to this week, this month, and who's going cold.

AI Copilot

Ask your network questions in plain English. Generate reconnection messages, meeting briefs, and smart tag suggestions.

Calendar & Email Sync

Connect Google or Outlook to automatically log meetings and emails. Pre-meeting briefings with full contact context.

Map View

See your contacts on an interactive map. Find nearby connections when traveling, manage locations in bulk.

Tags, Groups & Saved Views

Organize contacts with flexible tags and groups. Save custom filter combinations as views for quick access.

Smart Import

Import from Dex, Mesh, LinkedIn, Google Contacts, or any CSV. Auto-detects format and deduplicates intelligently.

Privacy First

Your data lives on your computer in a local database. Optional cloud sync for backup, but your contacts are always yours.
