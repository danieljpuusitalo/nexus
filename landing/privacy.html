Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What Data Nexus Collects

Locally stored data: All contact data you enter (names, emails, notes, interactions, etc.) is stored in a local SQLite database on your device. This is your primary copy and never leaves your machine unless you opt into cloud sync.

Cloud sync (optional): If you sign in with a Supabase account, your contact data is synced to a secure Supabase-hosted database so you can access it across devices. Cloud sync is entirely opt-in.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No selling or sharing of your data with third parties
  • No advertising or ad-based tracking
  • No telemetry sent to Nexus servers

AI Copilot

The AI Copilot feature uses your own Anthropic API key. API calls go directly from your device to Anthropic's servers using your key. Nexus does not proxy, store, or have access to your AI conversations. See Anthropic's privacy policy for how they handle API requests.

Google & Microsoft Integrations

If you connect Google or Microsoft accounts, Nexus accesses your calendar events and contacts through their official APIs using OAuth. Your Google/Microsoft credentials are never stored by Nexus — only OAuth tokens are kept locally on your device.

Chrome Extension

The Nexus Chrome extension reads publicly visible data from LinkedIn profile pages (name, company, headline) when you explicitly click "Save to Nexus." It does not collect browsing history, read other websites, or run in the background.

How to Delete Your Data

Go to Settings > Data Management > "Reset all data" to permanently delete all local data. If you use cloud sync, your cloud data will also be removed on the next sync cycle, or you can contact us to request immediate deletion.

Contact

For privacy questions, email: privacy@nexuscrm.app
